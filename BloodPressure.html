Blood Pressure

The root cause of BP is related to the emotions associated with the Heart and Blood vessels, such as impatience and hatred according to TCM(Traditional Chinese Medicine). For instance, a homemaker hastily multitasking to prepare her family to start the day is mostly impatient since she needs to meet the respective demands of the family members while maintaining punctuality. Trying to complete a task within a stipulated time makes the person impatient, leading to Hypertonic Heart resulting in Blood Pressure (BP). The goal of the treatment is to wean off impatience through flower remedies and lifestyle modifications, aiming to calm the mind.
